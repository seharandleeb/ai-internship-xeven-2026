## 3. Challenges Faced and How I Overcame Them

### Challenge: Difficulty Starting Tasks
The most consistent challenge I faced in Week 1 was not a code bug — it was
the mental barrier of getting started. At the beginning of each session, I
found it difficult to take the first step, especially when a task felt large
or unclear.

**How I resolved it:** I pushed through by committing to just writing the
first function or the first few lines, without worrying about the full
solution. Once I started, momentum followed naturally. This taught me a
professional lesson: in software development, starting imperfectly is always
better than waiting for perfect clarity. By the end of Week 1, I was
initiating tasks faster and with more confidence.
