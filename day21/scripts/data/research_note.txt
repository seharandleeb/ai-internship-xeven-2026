Research Note - Semantic Search Evaluation

Author: Sara Malik
Organization: Xeven Solutions
Date: 2026-02-09
Email: sara.malik@xeven.example.com

We benchmarked the all-MiniLM-L6-v2 embedding model against a larger encoder. The MiniLM model produced 384-dimensional vectors and ran fully offline. Retrieval quality was acceptable for short documents, with a small compute budget of $1,200 spent during the experiment.
